About Caring Heart

Caring Heart Family Nurse Practitioner (CHFNP) is a family practice started and owned by Evangeline Diaz Butler, a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner in Palm Coast, FL. We work collaboratively with other healthcare professionals to provide optimum care to our patients.

We proudly serve the Palm Coast area and neighboring cities — and extend our affordable healthcare services statewide through Telehealth.

We warmly welcome you to our family practice and express gratitude for selecting Caring Heart as your primary care provider.
