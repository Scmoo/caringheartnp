Caring Heart Family Nurse Practitioner (“Caring Heart,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard the information you provide when interacting with our practice, website, and communications.

By using our services, website, or opting into our communications, you agree to the terms described in this policy. This policy is intended to meet applicable requirements including the FCC’s TCPA rules governing telephone and SMS communications.

Section 01

Overview

Caring Heart Family Nurse Practitioner is a primary care practice located at 21 Old Kings Rd N, Unit 108, Palm Coast, FL 32137. We provide healthcare services to patients in the state of Florida.

This policy applies to all information collected through our website, phone interactions, patient intake processes, appointment scheduling systems, and any electronic communications including SMS/text messaging.

Section 02

SMS & Text Messaging Policy

Caring Heart may use SMS (text message) communications to contact patients regarding appointment reminders, scheduling confirmations, general health information, and practice updates. Your participation in SMS communications is voluntary and requires your explicit consent.

FCC Compliance Statement

No phone or mobile number information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of data described in this policy exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent — this information will not be shared with any third parties.

SMS Data Sharing Notice

We will not sell, share, or rent your SMS opt-in information to any third party for any reason other than to deliver the specific services associated with the campaign. However, we may share your personal data, including your SMS opt-in or consent status, with third parties that assist us in providing messaging services — such as platform providers, phone carriers, and other vendors involved in delivering text messages.

To opt out of SMS communications from Caring Heart at any time, reply STOP to any text message. To re-subscribe, reply START. For assistance, reply HELP or contact our office directly.

Section 03

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers only as necessary to operate our practice and deliver care. These parties are contractually obligated to protect your information and may not use it for any other purpose.

Third parties we may work with include:

  • Electronic Health Record (EHR) and appointment scheduling platforms (e.g., Elation EMR)
  • SMS and VOIP telecommunications service providers for appointment communications
  • Medical billing partners (e.g., Accurate Billing LLC) for insurance and claims processing
  • Phone carriers and messaging infrastructure vendors involved in text message delivery
  • Government agencies or law enforcement when required by applicable law

We never share your information with third parties for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

Section 04

Information We Collect

To provide you with care and communicate effectively, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: name, phone number, email address, and mailing address
  • Appointment & Scheduling Data: preferred visit times, visit type (office, home, telehealth), and appointment history
  • Insurance Information: insurance carrier, policy number, and coverage details for billing purposes
  • Health Information: collected separately under our HIPAA-compliant Notice of Privacy Practices (see Section 08)
  • Communications Data: records of SMS opt-in consent, phone call notes, and email correspondence
  • Website Usage: basic analytics data if you visit our website (e.g., pages visited, browser type)
Section 05

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling, confirming, and managing your appointments
  • Sending appointment reminders and follow-up communications via phone, text, or email
  • Processing insurance claims and coordinating billing through our billing partners
  • Delivering telehealth or remote care services
  • Coordinating care with specialists, facilities, or other providers you have been referred to
  • Complying with legal obligations, including healthcare regulations and law enforcement requests
  • Improving our practice operations and communications

We will only use your information for the purposes for which it was collected, or for closely related compatible purposes.

Section 06

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include secure data storage, restricted access controls, and use of HIPAA-compliant platforms for managing patient communications.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us promptly if you believe your information may have been compromised.

Section 07

HIPAA & Health Information

As a healthcare provider, Caring Heart Family Nurse Practitioner is subject to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Your protected health information (PHI) is governed by our separate Notice of Privacy Practices, which you will receive at your first visit or upon request.

Our Notice of Privacy Practices describes in detail how we use and disclose your medical information, your rights as a patient, and our legal obligations. This Privacy Policy is a supplement to — and does not replace — that notice.

If you have questions about how your health information is handled, please contact our office directly.

Section 08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. When we make changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. If you have questions about any changes, please contact us.
